Yuan Longping

The Father of Hybrid Rice.

"I saw rice plants as tall as Chinese sorghum,” said Yuan Longping

a dream he once had, “each ear of rice as big as a broom and each

grain of rice as huge as a peanut. I could hide in the shadow of the

rice crops with a friend." Yuan was just awarded 5-million-yuan State

Supreme Science and Technology Prize for his high yield hybrid rice species.

This award is viewed as "Chinese Nobel Prize".
